Chewing the Bone

Chewing on dog chew bones or hard toys helps scrape away plaque, which can cause bad breath and a number of potentially serioous dental problems. The action of the bone against the teeth scrapes away plaque and controls tartar buildup - helping prevent periodontal disease and yellowing of teeth.
